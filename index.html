AI Builder Evaluation Workbench

Upload a resume PDF to extract evidence across six AI Builder capability axes, generate quantitative scores, and support reviewer calibration.

Resume-grounded RAGRetrieves relevant resume evidence for each evaluation axis before scoring.
6-axis scoringScores each axis from 0 to 10 and visualizes the result as a radar chart.
Human calibrationLets reviewers adjust uncertain scores and document their reasoning.

Six Evaluation Axes

Each axis is scored from 0 to 10 using resume-based evidence, not school names or employer brand signals.

Problem Framing

Looks for evidence of defining ambiguous problems, MVP scope, success criteria, and trade-offs.

Practical Building

Looks for working artifacts such as prototypes, automations, APIs, dashboards, and workflows.

AI Leverage

Looks at how LLMs, RAG, agents, and prompt evaluation were applied to real workflows.

Enterprise Judgment

Checks whether security, privacy, governance, human approval, and operational risk were considered.

Stakeholder Thinking

Looks for evidence of balancing business, IT, legal, risk, and user needs.

Communication & Growth

Looks for clear communication, learning agility, documentation, demos, and onboarding evidence.

Evaluation Pipeline

AI supports evidence extraction and organization, while final judgment and calibration remain human-owned.

1
PDF Intake

Extracts text from the resume PDF and splits it into analyzable chunks.

2
Evidence Retrieval

Finds relevant resume evidence for each of the six evaluation axes.

3
Scoring

Scores evidence based on specificity, ownership, impact, and enterprise relevance.

4
Comparison Set

Compares the candidate with other synthetic examples on the same axis.

5
Human Calibration

Allows reviewers to adjust scores, add notes, and define follow-up questions.

This is a frontend prototype for demonstration. In production, API keys and resume text should be handled through a secure server-side proxy such as a Netlify Function.
